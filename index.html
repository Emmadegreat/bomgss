OUR HISTORY

The School started on 9th September 2018 and the pioneer Manager and his wife are Ven Engr Kenechukwu Obinna & Mrs Ngozi Rose Onyemaobim who is serving as the Vicar from 13th January 2017 till date and the school was named after the incubent Diocesan bishop in the person of Rt Rev Monday Chukwuma Nkwoagu JP. Like a mustard seed the School started with very few students is growing to the glory of God.
The Pioneer and the serving Principal is in the person of Mrs Tina Ahawike. She was posted to the new School by His Lordship Rt Rev Monday Chukwuma Nkwoagu JP to serve as the Principal.

The pioneer teachers include Mr Chukwuma Mgbebu, Chidinma Oluebube Onyemaobim, Nmesoma Chiamaka Onyemaobim, Edwin Okechukwu a Corps Member, an Ivorian by name; Lawrence who was handling the French classes, Pastor Nnamdi Ihetu, Mrs Riches Efeomo now the Dean of Studies. Mrs Eucharia Onyebuchi latter joined the teaching crew. The Teaching Crew had since grown in number and qualifications overtime.
The pioneer Senior Prefect of the School is in the person of Master Izuchukwu Israel Nwafor.

Our Facilities

Experience a transformative education at Bishop Okoye Memorial Girls' Secondary School Ukpor, where we offer innovative learning solutions alongside top-notch facilities. From advanced science labs to a vast library and a contemporary computer lab, our state-of-the-art amenities enhance your academic journey. Join us and elevate your learning experience to new heights!

Computer Lab

Our secondary school computer lab is a state-of-the-art facility equipped with cutting-edge technology. It provides students with hands-on experience in coding, programming, and digital literacy, fostering a dynamic learning environment for the exploration of modern technology.

Science Lab

Our science laboratory is a hub for scientific exploration. It is well-equipped with advanced apparatus, enabling students to engage in hands-on experiments across physics, chemistry, and biology. The lab promotes inquiry-based learning, encouraging students to develop critical thinking skills..

Library

The library is a quiet haven for intellectual growth. Stocked with a diverse collection of books, it offers students a tranquil space for research and independent study. Our library serves as a resourceful center, nurturing a love for reading and fostering a culture of lifelong learning.
